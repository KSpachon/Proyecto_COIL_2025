Colombia & Universidad de los Llanos

Colombia is a megadiverse, joyful and welcoming country. We are the second most biodiverse nation in the world, with two oceans, the Amazon rainforest, the Andes mountains and the Eastern Plains (Llanos Orientales).

Universidad de los Llanos (Unillanos), located in Villavicencio, Meta, is a public university founded in 1974. It is the leading institution in the Colombian Orinoquía region and trains professionals committed to the sustainable development of the Llanos.

Llanera & Colombian Culture

Traditions that unite us

Mamona (Llanera-style beef)

Beef slowly grilled on stakes over an open fire. Served with yuca, salted potatoes and guacamole. A true symbol of Llanera hospitality!

Joropo

Traditional dance with harp, cuatro and maracas. Fast footwork and spins. Celebrated at festivals such as the Festival del Retorno.

Coleo

Cowboys chase and bring down bulls by grabbing their tails at full speed. A sport of bravery and cattle-herding tradition.

Bandeja Paisa

Iconic dish with beans, rice, pork belly, fried egg, avocado and arepa. A real explosion of flavor!
